Colour story

Deep navy and instrument blue come from the control room — serious, trusted, technical. Flare orange is the warning light, the signal that demands attention. Prairie white keeps it readable and grounded. Midnight is the foundation everything sits on.

Name origin
VIXMOR is a compound of Victor and Morris — the two grandfathers. Kenneth, the father, and Brent, the founder, complete the four generations represented in the node mark. The name is owned, distinctive, and carries no prior meaning — a clean slate built on family.
